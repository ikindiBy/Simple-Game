COSMO

Short description

You are a cosmos discoverer on a recently opened planet.

  • Your task is to save your live
  • collect gold discs as many as possible
  • find key to secret station and get to it
  • But one more condition:
  • your space suit has limited an oxygen supply
  • Don't waste time, you have only 60 items of time
  • By the way:
  • if you collect more than 60 discs
  • you will get one more life!
